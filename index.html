Our Rich Heritage

Founded in 1902 by a group of cricket enthusiasts, VVV (Veni Vidi Vici) Cricket Club has grown from humble beginnings to become one of the Amsterdam's most respected cricket club.

Our pristine ground have hosted countless memorable matches, from junior development games to senior championship finals.

Today, we continue to uphold the traditions that make cricket special while embracing modern training methods and facilities.
