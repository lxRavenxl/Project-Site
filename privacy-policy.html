Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

VVV (Veni Vidi Vici) Cricket Club (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

  • Cookies and tracking data to enhance your browsing experience.
  • Analytics data such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited collected via Google Analytics.
  • Personal data you provide, such as your name and email, if you fill out forms or contact us directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to improve our website, provide analytics, personalize content and ads, and communicate with you if you request it.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, analyze site performance, and deliver personalized ads via Google and other partners. You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

4. Google Services and Consent

We use Google services (Google Analytics, Ads, and related tools), which may process your data for analytics and ad personalization. By using our site, you consent to this data processing. You can opt out of personalized ads by visiting Google Ads Settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third-party providers (like Google) solely to help us run analytics, advertising, or other services for the site.

6. Your Rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data; object to or restrict processing; and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at bestuur@vvv-cricket.nl.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal obligations.

8. Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: bestuur@vvv-cricket.nl.
